Enabling 11

This exercise follows the basic rules of a tic-tac-toe game. Clicking the play button in any of the nine boxes activates a German video. Select the corresponding German phrase from the drop-down menu that will appear. If you are correct, an O will appear in the box, if not, an X will appear. Your goal is to beat the computer by creating a row of three O's in any direction (horizontally, vertically or diagonally).
